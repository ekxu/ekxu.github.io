Otterz is a fintech platform built for startup founders and new business owners. Designed to be intuitive and accessible, Otterz uses AI to help users understand financial trends and navigate key metrics without prior experience. The platform simplifies finance management, guiding users through cash flow, budgeting, and financial insights.

The Problem

Otterz’s financial dashboard offers limited customization options, restricting users from tailoring their view to align with specific business needs. Additionally, a lack of clear visual hierarchy makes it challenging for users to quickly identify essential financial insights.

From further user research, inconsistent readability due to font and layout choices was identified as another barrier, complicating navigation and reducing the dashboard’s effectiveness for new business owners.

The Solution

We designed Otterz to provide users with real-time financial tracking through hourly updates, seamlessly integrating AI with QuickBooks to centralize and explain company finances. By connecting their QuickBooks portfolio to Otterz, users can access insights that interpret financial trends, helping them understand why metrics might have increased or decreased. The platform also offers a flexible dashboard, allowing users to customize which insights are displayed based on their priorities.

The Impact

Following the design implementation, user retention increased by 35%, with a 50% rise in new user sign-ups, demonstrating increased reliance on Otterz for business analytics and decision-making support.
