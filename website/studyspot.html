StudySpot is a campus-based app designed to streamline the process of finding available study spaces at UC Berkeley. By providing real-time seating availability across multiple libraries, StudySpot saves students time and enhances their productivity by allowing them to quickly locate the ideal study spot without the hassle of checking each library in person.
