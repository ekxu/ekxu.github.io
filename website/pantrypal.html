Pantry Pal is a campus-based app designed to make the UC Berkeley Food Pantry more accessible and efficient. By providing real-time inventory updates, appointment scheduling, and grocery pickup options, Pantry Pal saves students time and reduces uncertainty around pantry visits. The app helps students plan their trips, avoid long waits, and ensure they get the groceries they need.
